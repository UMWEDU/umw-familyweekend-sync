<h3>Tour of Brompton, Home of the UMW President</h3>
<ul>
 	<li>3 p.m. - 5 p.m., Historic Brompton</li>
</ul>
Described as one of America’s most historic houses, Brompton is famous for being at the center of the Battle of Fredericksburg in 1862. The home now serves as the official residence of the University president. President and Mrs. Paino will be on hand to greet families. Please access the house via the walkway on Hanover Street, across from the Jepson Alumni Executive Center located at 1119 Hanover Street.

<h3>UMW Art Galleries</h3>
The UMW Galleries presents two exhibitions.  The Ridderhof Martin Gallery features "The French Horse, from Romanticism to Surrealism: Works from the Virginia Museum of Fine Arts" with works by artists such as Degas, Delacroix, Gericault and more.

duPont Gallery features "Jon McMillan: Confluence" with works by UMW Assistant Professor of ceramics Jon McMillan.  Both exhibitions are on view September 1 - October 9.

Family weekend hours for both galleries: Friday (Sept 16) 10am - 4pm, Saturday (Sept 17) 10am - 4pm, and Sunday (Sept 18) 10am - 4pm.
